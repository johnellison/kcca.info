Privacy Statement

Keep Custer County Affordable

Keep Custer County Affordable respects your privacy. This statement explains how we handle information collected through our website.

Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect only the information you provide:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Mailing address (optional)
  • Employment status (optional)
  • Campaign material requests

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send you updates about the Keep Custer County Affordable campaign
  • Deliver requested campaign materials (yard signs, posters)
  • Keep you informed about Question 1A and related issues

We Will Never:

  • Sell your information to third parties
  • Share your information with other organizations
  • Use your information for purposes unrelated to this campaign

Unsubscribe Anytime

You can stop receiving communications from us at any time by sending an email to hello@kcca.info with "UNSUBSCRIBE" in the subject line. We will promptly remove you from our contact list.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Questions?

If you have questions about this privacy statement or how we handle your information, please contact us at hello@kcca.info.
